A web-based hotel management and reservation system designed to streamline hotel operations and enhance customer experience.

The platform allows customers to book rooms, manage reservations, explore dining options, place takeaway orders, and access hotel services through a centralized interface.

## Features

### Room Management
- Hotel room browsing
- Online room reservations
- Reservation processing
- Booking cancellation

### Dining Services
- Restaurant menu browsing
- Dining reservations
- Food ordering system

### Takeaway Services
- Online takeaway ordering
- Order management

### Customer Services
- Contact and inquiry pages
- Reviews and feedback section
